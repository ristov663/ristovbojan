The Faculty of Computer Science and Engineering (FCSE) is a result of the merger of the two largest institutes in the field of informatics and computer technology in Macedonia. As a result of the excellent efforts of the members of the Institutes, and in trend with the world rise of informatics, there is a constant growth of the scientific and technical capacities of the Institutes and increasing interest of the students in this field.

Throughout the development period, there are a number of mutual projects and researches, both on a personal and institutional basis. The constant race of the Institutes for the highest quality of studies, scientific research and application activity resulted in one final unifying factor - more significant development and rise of informatics in Macedonia and the formation of an extremely strong community of IT-oriented teaching staff. From 2011 they formally start walking together as the strongest and most advanced scientific - research and educational institution in Macedonia in the field of informatics - Faculty of Computer Science and Engineering (FCSE).
